Our Story

A little corner of Japan on Birch Street

Set just off California Avenue, Daigo is a small, unhurried sushi bar built around a single idea: let exceptional fish speak for itself. Slide up to the counter and you'll watch each piece shaped to order, the chef talking you through where the catch came from and how to enjoy it.

The room is warm and easygoing — equally suited to a quiet date night or a relaxed gathering with friends. With counter seats, a handful of tables, and dog-friendly seating outside, everyone is welcome here. What keeps regulars coming back is the rare combination Daigo manages to strike: serious, market-fresh sushi served with genuine warmth, and prices that feel fair for Palo Alto.

The Counter Experience

Omakase — leave it to the chef

Our signature tasting is a daily journey through the best fish to arrive that morning. Each course is composed at the counter to spotlight the seafood at its peak, opening with a silky chawanmushi egg custard and closing with house-made yuzu cheesecake.

$110 / full experience

Prefer something shorter? Ask about our 12-piece omakase, finished with your choice of uni or wagyu and a hand roll.
